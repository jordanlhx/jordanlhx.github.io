Biography

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter. He was discovered by a talent manager via his YouTube videos in 2008. Since then, he has become one of the world's best-selling music artists.

Fun Facts About Justin Bieber

  • Justin Bieber was discovered on YouTube by talent manager Scooter Braun when he was just 13 years old.
  • His first single, "One Time," was released in 2009 and became a worldwide hit.
  • He has won numerous awards, including Grammy Awards, American Music Awards, and Billboard Music Awards.
  • Justin is the first artist to have seven songs from a debut album chart on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • He has a tattoo of a seagull on his hip, which he got when he was 16 years old.
  • Justin Bieber's favorite color is purple, and he often wears it in his outfits.
  • He has over 200 million followers on Instagram, making him one of the most followed celebrities on the platform.
